📌 What is TPB?

Turbo Paper Boat is a DeFi vault token backed by real BTC. Deposit WBTC, receive TPB tokens priced at NAV. Your Bitcoin works — you don't.

⚙️ How It Works

The strategy runs in cycles (ATH → ATH). On the way down, the vault accumulates more BTC using leveraged grid shorts on Deribit + Aave borrowing. On the way up, shorts generate contango yield.

🏆 NFT Rewards

Hold TPB through cycles to earn NFT bonuses (Bronze → Silver → Gold → Diamond). NFTs multiply your share of rewards at each cycle end. Trade them freely.
